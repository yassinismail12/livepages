Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Travel Go handles information when users interact with the automated messaging assistant.

Data We Process

Messages sent to a connected Facebook Page or Instagram Business account may be processed to generate automated replies.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect Facebook profile data such as email, friends list, birthday, or gender. We do not use Facebook Login.

Use of Meta APIs

The Service uses Meta Messaging APIs solely to send and receive Direct Messages on behalf of connected business accounts.

Data Retention

Message data may be stored temporarily for service functionality and troubleshooting.
