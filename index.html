Travel Go Messaging Assistant

Automated Facebook & Instagram Direct Message Assistant

Travel Go is an automated messaging assistant designed to help businesses reply to customer inquiries through Facebook Page Direct Messages and Instagram Direct Messages.

The assistant responds automatically based on predefined business data and rules. No Facebook Login is used, and no personal profile data is accessed.

This website is provided for transparency and to share legal documentation related to the service.
